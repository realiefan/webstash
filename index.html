This app can't run in a browser, you need to install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

    For Android, PC, Mac Users:
  1. Open the app in the Chrome browser.
  2. Tap the three dots in the top-right corner.
  3. Customize the app name if needed and tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install" .

    For iOS, iPad, Safari Users:
  1. On Safari, tap the Share icon at the bottom of the Safari browser.
  2. Scroll down and tap on "Add to Home Screen" .
  3. Customize the app name if needed and tap "Add" .

    After installing this App, you will be able to organize, manage, and run all your WebApp in one place in a secure and trustless manner.
