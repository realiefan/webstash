This app can't run in a browser, you need to install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

    For IOS, iPad, Safari Users:
  • On Safari, Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the Safari browser.
  • Scroll down and tap on "Add to Home Screen".
  • Customize the app name if needed and tap "Add".
      • For Android, PC, Mac users:
  • Open the app in the Chrome browser
  • Tap the three dots in the top-right corner.
  • Customize the app name if needed and tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install".
